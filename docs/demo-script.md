# Demo Script

1. Open the home page and explain the product in one sentence: Web3 stamp-card loyalty for real-world visits.
2. Open `/app`, connect a wallet, optionally add a profile name, and create a loyalty program with an HTTPS icon URL.
3. Open the manager view and show the printed visit QR actions: copy, share, download, and print sheet.
4. Open the fixed QR as a customer, collect one static stamp, and confirm immediate reuse is blocked.
5. Generate a live QR, point out the five-minute countdown, scan it as a customer, and collect a stamp instantly.
6. Regenerate the live QR to show the owner-led counter workflow.
7. Issue enough stamps to reach the threshold.
8. Claim the reward from the customer card.
9. Open `/app/claim/[claimId]`, show the QR, backup code, and owner-wallet validation hint.
10. Validate the claim as owner and confirm that a second validation fails.
11. End on the dashboard showing the customer card, program manager, and claim history.
